About Me

I'm a nuclear engineer turned software engineer

Ever since I was a child, I have always been tinkering or interested in technology in some capacity. In recent years, I started building side projects and talking to friends in the tech industry. Through these conversations and inspired by my interest in entrepreneurship and technology, I decided to enroll in App Academy, a rigorous software development program.

During those intense 4 months, I spent 80-100 hours every week and learned multiple coding languages and frameworks. These include Ruby on Rails, JavasScript, and React, among others. The experience has prepared me to learn any language and framework extremely quickly. This experience has made me a versatile software engineer with a growth mindset. I am excited to apply my skillset and passion for problem-solving to a purpose and value-driven team.

Smooth is an intuitive trip planning application built with the MERN stack. Users can search for activities/experiences based on location, price, and time available. Users can then drag and drop those experiences into their itinerary.
